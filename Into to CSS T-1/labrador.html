Labrador

Description:

The Labrador Retriever or simply Labrador is a British breed of retriever gun dog. It was developed in the United Kingdom from St. John's water dogs imported from the colony of Newfoundland, and was named after the Labrador region of that colony

Characteristics:

Labradors are playful and intelligent, with a warm, friendly temperament that makes them ideal for first-time owners. Labradors are easy-going, rewarding pets with high energy levels, meaning they love extra attention and exercise. They're highly affectionate, easily trainable, aim to please, and love to play. However, as extremely loving people-pleasers, Labs tend to have a high demand for attention and physical contact, which may be problematic for some if left alone for extended periods of time.

Appearance:

They can weigh between 55 and 85 pounds and stand up to 24 inches tall at the shoulder

They have a thick, double coat of short, water-repellent fur that comes in a variety of colors, including black, brown, yellow, cream, and white.
